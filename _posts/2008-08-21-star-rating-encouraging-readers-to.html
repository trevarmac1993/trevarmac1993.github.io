--- layout: post title: "Star rating - Encouraging readers to interact" date: 2008-08-21 comments: true tags: [ Blogging ] --- Although having quite a lot of blog readers per day, it is quite hard to make your readers interact with you. Now I've experimentally added "star-rating" which allows my readers to rate my posts, whether they were helpful or interesting or completely useless. In this way I hope to get some more feedback from my readers (so vote ;) ). Btw, thanks Blogger-In-Draft for this new feature!

Unfortunately I have to note that the star rating widget seems to cause some style-problems on Safari (for Windows). I'm sorry for that. I'll try to find a way to fix that as soon as possible.
