--- layout: post title: "Accidentally deleted the Ubuntu main menu bar!!" date: 2009-02-03 comments: true tags: [ Linux ] --- I just clicked accidentally on the main top panel on the Ubuntu desktop and damn...I deleted the menu bar...shock!!
But actually after some minutes of searching it's really easy to restore it actually. Just go on the top-panel, right-click and press "Add Panel". Then select "main menu" and add it again.
